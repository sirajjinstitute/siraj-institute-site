About Siraj

Vision & Mission

Who We Are

At Siraj Institute, we believe that the Quran is the only source of true light, and that the Sunnah of the Prophet ﷺ is its explanation and completion.

وَأَنزَلْنَا إِلَيْكُمْ نُورًا مُّبِينًا

Allah says about the Quran: "We have sent down to you a clear light."

وَسِرَاجًا مُّنِيرًا

And He says about His Prophet ﷺ: "and as a lamp, giving light."

From this perspective, we established Siraj (The Lamp) to bring people closer to the Book of Allah by teaching correct recitation and sound understanding.

Our Mission

Bringing the light of the Quran to every family through clear, structured, and authentic Quran and Arabic teaching, delivered via premium 1-on-1 online sessions and interactive video lessons.

Our Vision & Ultimate Goal

Knowledge transformed into action. Our ultimate goal is to see our students live by what they learn. We ask Allah to grant us, and everyone who learns His Book through us, absolute sincerity (Ikhlas) and success in applying it to daily life.

Programs

Four roadmaps, one destination

Every path is broken into phases and steps, so progress is always visible.

The Ultimate Quran & Tajweed Roadmap — from first letter to a full Khatmah of recitation, and memorization for those who choose it.

Phase 1 — The Foundational Phase 2.5–3 months total
1
4–5 weeks · ~12–15 classes
Arabic Alphabet Mastery

Pronouncing letters from their correct articulation points, recognizing letter shapes (beginning, middle, end of word), and mastering short and long vowels (Fatha, Damma, Kasra, and Madd).

2
2 weeks · ~6 classes
Reading Full Words

Combining letters to read complete words smoothly, introducing Sukoon and Tanween.

3
2–3 weeks · ~8 classes
Reading Full Sentences

Moving from single-word reading to full, compound sentences, preparing the student to open the Mushaf.

Phase 2 — Quranic Fluency & Tajweed 3–4 months total
4
1.5 months · ~18 classes
Intro to Tajweed & Quran Reading

The student begins reading directly from the Mushaf alongside a practical Tajweed course focused on essential rules and correct articulation points.

5
2–2.5 months · ~24 classes
Completing Juz Amma & Basic Tajweed

Completing recitation (or memorization, if desired) of Juz Amma while fully mastering the essential Tajweed rules (Madd, Ghunnah, Qalqalah, Raa rules), enabling independent reading anywhere in the Quran.

Phase 3 — The Mastery & Completion Phase
6
~2–2.5 years · 3 classes/week
Full Quran Recitation Khatmah

The student starts with one Rub' (a quarter of a Hizb — roughly 1/8 of a Juz) per class, gradually increasing as their level and reading speed improve.

7
Optional
Full Quran Memorization Khatmah

Intensive track: 2.5–3 years (1–2 new pages per class with review). Regular track: 4–5 years (½–1 page per class), suited to children and busy schedules.

Full Quran Tafsir Journey — understand the meanings of the surahs recited daily, learn the reasons for revelation (Asbab al-Nuzul), and apply the verses in daily life (Tadabbur). Tailored for kids & adults.

1
3–4 months
The Foundation & Juz Amma

Surah Al-Fatiha and the complete Juz Amma. Kids: word meanings, surah stories (Al-Feel, Quraish, Al-Masad), lessons learned. Adults: the purposes of the surahs, reasons for revelation, daily messages relevant to prayer.

2
4.5–5.5 months
Juz Tabarak & The Meccan Gems

Juz Tabarak and selected surahs with profound spiritual and narrative impact (Al-Kahf, Ya-Sin, Yusuf, Luqman, Al-Hujurat). Kids: faith through creation, the story in Yusuf, etiquette in Al-Hujurat. Adults: ethics, prophets' stories, intellectual resilience.

3
12–15 months
The Long Surahs & Islamic Legislation

The major surahs (Al-Baqarah, Aal Imran, An-Nisa, Al-Ma'idah, Al-An'am, Al-A'raf, Al-Anfal, At-Tawbah). Kids: the major stories and their values. Adults: Islamic legislation, transactions, family building, jurisprudence of the Seerah.

4
12–14 months
Completion & Graduation

Tafsir of the remaining parts of the Quran (Surah Yunus to Surah Al-Hadid). The student completes the Tafsir of the entire Quran with understanding and reflection, and receives the "Certificate of Completing the Simplified Tafsir of the Book of Allah" from Siraj Institute.

"One Course, Two Approaches: For kids, we use interactive storytelling and simplified visual aids. For adults, we focus on deep spiritual insights, context (Asbab al-Nuzul), and real-life applications."

Understanding sentence structure (nominal and verbal sentences, past and present tense) and expressing ideas in short, error-free paragraphs.

~3 classes/week · ~48–78 classes
Advanced Comprehension & Fluency

Reading complete stories and books, listening to and understanding programs, speaking fluently and confidently on general topics, and appreciating the beauty of Arabic rhetoric.

FAQs

Questions, answered

How do the classes take place?+

All our classes are conducted online via Zoom. They are live, interactive, and strictly 1-on-1 to ensure your teacher's full attention is dedicated entirely to you or your child.

If I sign up for a Family & Friends discount, will we study in a group?+

No, absolutely not. Even if you join our group discount plans to save on costs, every student enjoys a completely private schedule, a customized curriculum, and a dedicated teacher. Your classes remain 100% individual (1v1).

What happens if I or my child miss a class?+

We understand that life happens. Siraj Institute offers a highly flexible rescheduling policy. Upon finalizing your enrollment, you will receive a digital copy of our "Family Guide," which outlines all your rights and details our friendly options for rescheduling and making up missed classes.

Who are the tutors at Siraj Institute?+

Our team consists of highly qualified, native Arabic-speaking tutors, including prestigious Al-Azhar University graduates and certified Quran experts who are specially trained to teach non-native speakers and hold patient, engaging sessions for children (including those with ADHD).

Do I need to pay anything before the trial class?+

No. Your free trial class is 100% free and comes with zero commitment. It is designed to evaluate the student's level accurately and let you experience our teaching style firsthand before making any payment decision.
